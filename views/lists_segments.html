Create segment

A Segment is a subset of contacts in a list. Segments help you target your message to a specific recipient group. When you create a segment and attach it to a text message, the message is sent only to the contacts that meet the criteria of the segment (which is generated at the time of sending). These can include demographic restrictions (for example, only contacts with the last name Smith) or activity restrictions (for example, only contacts who joined a list on a specific date).

Build your segments based on the following criteria:

  • Create date (date they joined the list)
  • Modify date (date that the contact was modified)
  • Any of the 5 custom fields and demographic contact data
