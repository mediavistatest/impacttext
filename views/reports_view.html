You can create four types of scheduled reports:

Daily – Runs daily and covers a 24-hour period previous to the run time. For example, a report set to run daily at 12:00AM will cover the period from 12:00AM to 11:59PM the previous day.

Weekly – Runs once a week on the day and time you choose and covers the previous 7-day period. For example, a report set to run weekly at 12:00AM will cover the 7 previous days beginning at 12:00AM on Day 1 and ending at 11:59PM on Day 7.

Monthly - Runs once a month on the day and time you choose and covers the previous 30-day period. For example, a report set to run monthly at 12:00AM will cover the 30 previous days beginning at 12:00AM on Day 1 and ending at 11:59PM on Day 30.

Custom – Runs one time, at the time and date you select with a custom date range.

All reports will continue into the future. To stop a report, go to Manage and delete the report you want to end.
