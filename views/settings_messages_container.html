Email to SMS

Did you know you can send also text messages from your email address? First you must register the email addresses from which you would like enable text message sending.

Emails from registered email addresses must be sent to the following email address:

EmailtoText@CentralMessage.com

Please follow these instructions to set up your email to SMS.

The email subject must contain your Email to SMS Key, which can be found below.

Line 1 must be the SMS code from which you are sending the text message.

Line 2 must be the telephone number(s) you are sending the text to. You can send to up to 10 telephone numbers, separated by commas.

All other lines of the email will be treated as the text of the message, beginning with line 3. Please do not insert any blank lines.
